Executive Summary

The Ebttikar-OIP (Operational & Intelligence Platform) is an enterprise-grade IT Service Management system designed for comprehensive ticketing, team management, and operational excellence in Saudi Arabia.

The system encompasses 7 development phases covering core functionality through advanced AI capabilities. Starting with team management dashboards and SLA tracking, the platform evolves to include inventory management, AI-driven analytics, collaboration tools, and mobile applications.

Key features include Operations Dashboard with live ticket monitoring, SLA Performance tracking with breach predictions, Resource Management for workforce planning, Inventory System with barcode generation, and AI Chatbots for intelligent query resolution. The platform supports multi-project configurations, role-based permissions, and integrates with existing enterprise systems.
